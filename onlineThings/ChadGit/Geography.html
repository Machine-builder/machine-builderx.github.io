Xi Jinping (China)

By choosing the Chinese advisor, Chad joins the One Belt One Road initiative, making infrastructure cheaper to build.

The United States of America, through the US Aid is the largest supplier of foreign aid to Chad, spending over sixty-million dollars on aid annually. By choosing the American advisor you give chad greater access to American aid, as a result droughts become less likely and you get food shipments.
